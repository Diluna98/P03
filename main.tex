\section{Circuit calculation}
Calculated the Voltages on the Resistors R1 and R2 in DC circuit using Voltage Division Rule.

\[V_{R1} =(V_{1} \times R_{1})/(R_{1} + R_{2})
\]
\[V_{R2} =(V_{1} \times R_{2})/(R_{1} + R_{2})
\]
